Sustainable WordPress Development

A sustainably oriented (WordPress) web developer can help make digitalization more ecological and program beautiful and fast, economically sustainable websites with little maintenance and long-lasting open-source technology.

WordPress celebrated its 20th birthday in 2023, forming the technical basis for millions of websites. Thanks to the numerous free training options, it is easy to find employees who can maintain and extend a website's content without you having to learn it yourself or hire professional programmers.

Focus, maintainability and long life

Web development (programming) should be limited to programming modular components (themes and plugins) that have a long lifespan and can be easily customised and extended. As an alternative to the popular patchwork of freely available plugins, individual programming can create a sustainable basis that is elegant, efficient and accessible right from the start. Web developers can also give advice on the selection and structure (architecture) of software components and subsequently optimise existing WordPress websites.
