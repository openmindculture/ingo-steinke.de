Hi, I'm a Frontend Web Developer in Berlin!

I'm glad you found me! I'm Ingo Steinke, a freelance web developer with more than 25 years of professional experience. Currently, I prefer to work on frontend projects with a focus on accessibility, mobile/responsive layout, optimizing loading speed, efficiency and ecological sustainability.

As an experienced freelancer, I complement developer teams remotely or on premise and help to implement accessible and performant websites in a secure, maintainable and future-proof way. I am familiar with TypeScript, JavaScript, React, Astro and other frontend frameworks and libraries, Tailwind, CSS and testing tools.

Web Development in Berlin: Questions and Answers

Who creates websites in Berlin?

Berlin-based web developers like Ingo Steinke (that's me) create websites and web apps for customers in Berlin and everywhere else, no matter where you are. As a web developer from Berlin, I can quickly attend meetings in person or work in your office with your team. If requested early enough and subject to availability, I am also willing to travel outside Berlin.

How to find good web developers for your project team?

As a web developer, I have a strong network that extends beyond Berlin: designers, agencies, other web developers and specialised service providers are all part of my network. You can discover web developers in Berlin or in your area through my network, among other places./p>

How quickly can we start working together?

As an experienced web developer, I know why it is important to understand the mission and corporate culture and integrate into a team. As a freelance web developer, I must be able to achieve this quickly and on short notice. For the technical onboarding as well as getting to know each other I can also come around and join your team on site (in or outside of Berlin) if required.

How do you know whether I'm a good fit for your team as a freelancer?

In a non-binding talk, we can quickly find out if I can complement your team on a professional and personal level.

I am communicative, flexible and adaptable. I can also think beyond a requirement's wording if desired and expedient, in order to ask important questions in good time.

Why I work in Berlin as a Web Developer

Why Berlin? In Berlin, there are startups, meetups, projects, partners and the diverse infrastructure of the city. Coworking and exchange at the Impact Hub, Ignite Future Talks at CoCreation Loft, JavaScript- and accessibility meetups, Creative Mornings Berlin, beta Breakfast, conferences for creatives, designers and developers like Beyond Tellerrand, Green Coding and other sustainable events and initiatives.

Berlin also has excellent transport links, so I can quickly reach my business partners in Karlsruhe, Dresden, Hamburg or Düsseldorf by train when conferences or meetings are scheduled there.

In Berlin, we have access to meeting rooms at Impact Hub Berlin when needed. Otherwise, I enjoy communicating online: tools such as video conferencing, email, Figma, GitHub, Slack and Jira enable us to work together efficiently and flexibly, while also being creative and personal.

Can you also book me as an individual client?

As a freelance web developer, I can complement your team. I also offer services to medium-sized enterprises and startups.

Customers with large projects, on the other hand, appreciate the advantages of interdisciplinary teams from agencies or networks.
